Quick Help

How to book a venue?

To book a venue, navigate to the Rooms page, select your preferred venue, check availability, and complete the booking form with your details.

Cancellation policy

Cancellations made 48 hours before the booking start time receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive 50% refund. No refunds for no-shows.

Payment options

We accept credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments through our secure payment gateway.

Technical support

For technical issues, please contact our support team via email or phone. We provide 24/7 assistance for urgent matters.
